About Ritviz

Crowned as the highest streamed Indian indie artist on Spotify in India, Ritviz is undeniably one of the most unique names in music to come out of the Indian subcontinent. With a number of chart-topping hits to his name - Udd Gaye, Sage & Liggi to name a few, Ritviz has managed to showcase a sound that is inimitable to say the least. Turning a new leaf in his artistic journey, Ritviz is set to make heads bob and feet dance uncontrollably with his debut full length album ‘Mimmi’ which released worldwide on September 2nd, 2022.
